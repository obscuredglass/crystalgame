The Crystal Selector and Collector

Here's how you play


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four colorful crystals below to choose from.
    • Sapphire
    • Emerald
    • Ruby
    • Amber
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a certain amount of points added to your total score.
  4. You win the game by exactly matching your total score to the random number given to you.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click your mouse on it.
  7. Each time when the game starts, the game will reset the values of each crystal to a completely different number.
